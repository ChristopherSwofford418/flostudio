Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Citation Shield: AI Verify ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Citation Shield: AI Verify does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • We do not require account registration or login.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers.
  • We do not store any citation text or queries you submit on our servers.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use advertising networks or analytics SDKs that collect personal data.

2. How the App Works

When you submit a citation for verification, the text is sent to an AI verification service solely for the purpose of checking the citation's accuracy. This transmission is:

  • Ephemeral: The text is processed in real time and is not retained after the response is returned.
  • Encrypted: All data transmitted between the App and the verification service is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/HTTPS protocols.
  • Not linked to you: No user identifier is associated with the submitted text.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses an AI-powered citation verification service to process your queries. This service processes text ephemerally and does not retain user data. We do not share your data with any other third parties for marketing, advertising, or analytics purposes.

4. Children's Privacy

Citation Shield: AI Verify is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted information through our App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any data transmitted through the App. All communications between the App and our servers are encrypted using TLS. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
