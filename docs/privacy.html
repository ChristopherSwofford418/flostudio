🛡️ Citation Shield — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Citation Shield ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by Christopher Swofford. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Citation Shield mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

Documents and Text You Submit: When you use Citation Shield to verify citations, you submit legal documents or text excerpts. This content is processed by our AI system to identify and verify citations. We do not permanently store the content of your documents beyond the duration of the verification session.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics such as number of scans performed, app version, and device type to improve the App. This data is not linked to your identity.

Subscription Information: If you subscribe to Citation Shield Pro or Law Firm plans, subscription management is handled by Apple through the App Store. We receive only a subscription status indicator — we do not receive your payment information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered citation verification services
  • To track your usage quota (free tier: 3 scans/month)
  • To improve the accuracy and performance of our citation verification AI
  • To respond to support requests

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Document content submitted for verification is processed by our AI infrastructure and is not shared with any third parties.

5. Data Retention

Scan history is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. You can delete your scan history at any time from within the App. Document content submitted for AI processing is not retained after the verification is complete.

6. Children's Privacy

Citation Shield is intended for legal professionals and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data transmission between the App and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.

8. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any data associated with your use of the App by contacting us at the email address below. You can also clear all local scan history directly within the App settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
